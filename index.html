About Me

I'm a Computer Engineer passionate about designing cutting-edge CMOS Integrated Circuits and embedded systems. My research focuses on developing innovative solutions for high-energy particle detectors, contributing to advancements in particle physics.

During my PhD at the University of Bergamo, I am developing and characterizing front-end circuits in 28nm CMOS technology, significantly improving detector performance. I have a strong interest in the interface between hardware and software, and I am gaining solid experience in programming microcontrollers.
